This is a little course I made to help those new to n8n. It has three parts:

- Upload a spreadsheet to n8n
- Change the data in the spreadsheet
- Download the spreadsheet

Pretty common stuff, but the n8n.io docs and courses may be a bit intimidating for those new to this.
Head over to [https://docs.n8n.io/](https://docs.n8n.io/) and check out the quickstarts as well!
